Privacy Policy for HODL Time

Last updated: May 5, 2025

At HODL Time, your privacy is important to us. This privacy policy outlines our data practices in the simplest terms:

Price data is retrieved using the Binance public API. While we do not send user data to Binance, your device may interact with their servers to retrieve market data. Please refer to Binance's privacy policy for more details.

We Do Not Collect Personal Data

  • HODL Time does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.
  • We do not require account creation, login, or any identifying information to use the app.

Local-Only Storage

  • All app preferences (like currency, theme, and price lock settings) are stored locally on your device.
  • This data is never sent to external servers or shared with third parties.

No Tracking or Analytics

  • We do not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or behavioral tracking tools.
